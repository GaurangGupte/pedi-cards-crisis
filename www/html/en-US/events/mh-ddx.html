Differential
  • Signs
    • ↑ HR, ↑ PaCO2 with ↑ sweep on CPB circuit
    • ↑ Lactate, ↓ MVO2. Hyperkalemia
    • Hyperthermia after termination of CPB
    • Rhabdomyolysis, ↑ CK, myoglobinuria, skeletal muscle rigidity, DIC
    • Hemodynamic instability that rapidly resolved after Dantrolene administration
  • Differential Diagnosis
    • Sepsis
    • NMS
    • Seratonin syndrome
    • Myopathy
    • Pheochromocytoma
