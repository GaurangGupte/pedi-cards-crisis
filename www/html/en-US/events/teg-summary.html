Summary
TEG measures the relative impedance to the rotational movement of a pin in a cup due to clot formation and breakdown. It is a point of care, functional assay to assess coagulopathy
  • Indications
    • Evaluate coagulopathy in a bleeding patient
    • Guide transfusion strategies in trauma, major surgery (cardiac surgery), obstetric hemorrhage, and massive transfusion
  • Interpretation
    • if R-time prolonged, consider giving plasma or PCC
    • if MA is low, consider giving platelets or cryoprecipitate
    • if LY30 greater than 3%, patient has hyperfibrinolysis, consider giving TXA
