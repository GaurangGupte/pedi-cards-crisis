Other Considerations
Limitations
  • Does not account for thrombocytopenia or platelet dysfunction which must be separately tested (e.g. antiplatelet drugs or von Willebrand disease)
  • Normal ranges vary slightly by age. One study found prolonged INTEM CT, shorter EXTEM CT, and overall shorter CFT, greater A10, and greater MCF in patients less than 3 months old compared to older (up to 16 years old)
