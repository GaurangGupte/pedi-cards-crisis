Treatment
  • Check
    • Transducer height
    • Arterial line pressure on CPB
    • Aortic cannula position
    • Adequate venous drainage
    • Cannula for kinking
    • Check arterial inflow connected to arterial cannula
    • Medications infusing (vasoactive agents vs. vasodilators)
    • Hematocrit
  • Actions
    • Re-zero arterial line
    • Echo - TEE or epicardial to look at cannula position
    • Reposition aortic cannula
    • Increase/decrease anesthetic
    • Add vasoactive agent if hypotensive
      • norepinephrine, phenylephrine, vasopressin
    • Add vasodilator if hypertensive
      • Nitroprusside, hydralazine, phentolamine
    • In hypotension, consider empiric steroids
    • Increase/decrease CPB flows
    • In unexplained, consider cooling more on CPB until source is found
