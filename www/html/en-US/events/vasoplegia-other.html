Other Considerations
  • During rewarming, peripheral arterial hypotension may not reflect central arterial hypotension. Check a central arterial pressure if there is a concern for refractory vasoplegia during rewarming
  • Thiamine, hydrocortisone, and ascorbic acid have been used in adults with VPS with anecdotal success
  • Methylene blue and hydroxycobalamin can be used in sequence in refractory cases
