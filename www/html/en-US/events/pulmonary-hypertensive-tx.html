Treatment
  • Stop procedure and/or stimulation if at all possible
  • Increase FiO2
  • Inhaled nitric oxide, inhaled/IV prostanoids
  • Give additional sedation (opioids or benzodiazepines), consider muscle relaxants
  • Correct hypercarbia
  • Administer sodium bicarbonate if metabolic acidosis
  • Inotropes/Vasopressors
    • Epinephrine/dopamine improve RV function but excessive tachycardia may be detrimental
    • Norepinephrine may improve SVR but may also raise PVR
    • Vasopressin may improve SVR, possibly lower PVR
    • Milrinone may lower PVR but hypotension possible
  • Consideration for other supportive strategies such as ECMO
