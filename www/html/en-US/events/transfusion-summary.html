Summary
Signs/Symptoms
  • Hypotension
  • Hypertension (TACO)
  • Tachycardia, Fever, Rash
  • Coagulopathy, Bleeding, DIC
  • Hemolysis, Drop in hemoglobin level
  • Hemoglobinuria
  • Pulmonary edema (TRALI, TACO), hypoxemia
  • Rash
Consequences
  • Intravscular hemolysis, coagulopathy, DIC
  • End organ damage, renal failure
  • Death
Summary
  • Ensure products are checked prior to transfusion
  • Use leukocyte-reduced products
  • Use washed products if available
  • If unwashed pRBC are not availabe, consider use of cell saver to wash pRBC
  • Be caution with rapid or large volume transfusion
