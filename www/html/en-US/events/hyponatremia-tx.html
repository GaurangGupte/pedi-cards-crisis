Treatment
  • Recheck sodium, send serum sample for formal labs
  • Review all fluids pt is receiving, including CPB prime
  • Severe hyponatremia or neurologic symptoms
    • Hypertonic saline 3% 1-2ml/kg/hr until Na>125
    • Loop diuretics (furosemide)
    • Sodium bicarbonate 0.5-1 mEq/kg boluses
  • Avoid rapid overcorrection: 0.5-1mEq/hr, < 6 mEq in 24 hr
    • Sequelae: central pontine myelinolysis, cerebral edema, seizures
  • If SIADH - treat underlying cause
  • If Addison's - treat with steroids
  • If proceeding with urgent case and pt hyponatremic, discuss with the perfusionist: may require addition of free water or 5% dextrose to lower prime sodium concentration
  • If contrast agents are to be administered (cardiac cath) consider frequent sampling to monitor for sodium
