Differential
Types of acute tranfusion reactions
  • Acute hemolytic
    • Timing: immediate
    • Cause: ABO incompatibility, clerical error
  • Allergic/Anaphylactic
    • Timing: less than 30 minutes
    • Cause: IgA deficient patient, recipient IgE antibodies vs. donor proteins
  • TRALI (transfusion related acute lung injury)
    • Timing: less than 12 hours
    • Causes: donor antibodies vs. recipient HLA antigens in the lung tissue, particularly plasm-containing products
  • TACO (transfusion associated circulation overload)
    • Timing: less than 12 hours
    • Cause: very rapid and/or large volume transfusion
  • Febrile
    • Timing: less than 30-90 minutes
    • Cause: donor cytokine release
  • Infections/Transfusion associated sepsis
    • Timing: less than 60 minutes
    • Cause: bacterial or viral contamination
