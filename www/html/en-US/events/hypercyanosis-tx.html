Treatment
    Without IV Access
    • Calm child if agitated
    • Knees to chest position to increase SVR
    • Supplemental O2
    • Sedation via intramuscular or intransal routes
    With IV Access (in addition to those considerations listed above)
    • IV fluids, 10-20 mL/kg
      • Consider use of pRBC if cyanosis does not resolve
    • Intravenous sedation (midazolam, fentanyl, morphine)
    • Phenylephrine 1-10 mcg/kg IV
    • Beta-blocking agents (usually preventative therapy)
    • May require intubation with paralysis, ensure adequate ventilation
