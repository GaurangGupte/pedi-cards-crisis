Other Considerations
    Special Considerations for Digoxin Toxicity
    • Digoxin blocks the sodium/potassium ATPase pump
    • Digoxin toxicity can cause hyperkalemia
    • EKG findings may include:
      • VT/VF
      • progressive bradycardia or heart block
      • prolonged PR interval
      • downsloping ST depression in inferolateral leads
    • Causes of digoxin toxicity
      • Hypokalemia, hypermagnesemia, hypercalcemia
      • Digoxin overdose
      • Drug interactions: verapamil, macrolides, and antifungals
    • Treatments
      • Normalize potassium
      • Avoid calcium (possibility of myocardial injury)
      • Digibind/Digifab (antibody)
