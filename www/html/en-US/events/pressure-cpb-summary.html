Summary
    Definitions
    • Perfusion pressure = MAP - CVP
    • Recommended CPB MAP Ranges by Age
      • Neonate (< 1 month): MAP 30 - 40 mmHg
      • Child (1-10 years): MAP 40 - 50 mmHg
      • Adolescent (10 - 16 years): MAP 50 - 70 mmHg
      • Adult (> 16 years): MAP 60-90 mmHg
    Summary
    • Monitor MAP, NIRS, lactates, and urine output on bypass
    • Alert team with any concerning changes
    • Communicate regarding medication administration
